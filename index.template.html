How it Works

Bicycle Bicycle provides a way for Bike riders to find safe bike routes.

When we make routes, we try to avoid:

  • Known Crash Hotspots
  • Roundabouts
  • Highspeed roads
And we try to route you along:
  • Bicycle Paths
  • Walkways (that are suitable for cyclists)
And we try send you past:
  • Parks
